Treat Ball

Product Information

There is no greater fun for your dog than a toy that delivers a dog treat as well! Providing both mental and physical stimulation the Cyber Rubber Treat Ball is interactive and made from non-toxic rubber delivering safe and enjoyable play for your dog.

The ball has a hollow centre and the treat can be inserted into the ball through a small hole in the base. The base is weighted so that when the ball comes to rest the treat is ready to eat.
